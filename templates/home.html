Welcome to ConstructConnect

ConstructConnect is a platform designed to connect builders, material suppliers, transport contractors, architects, and other building professionals with potential customers who are looking to build or renovate their homes or commercial properties.

Our platform simplifies the process of finding and hiring quality professionals, purchasing construction materials, and managing the logistics of construction projects.

We understand the importance of secure and convenient payments. That's why we have integrated various payment options, including mobile money and card payments, to streamline the payment process and provide a secure payment system for both customers and professionals.

Our primary focus is on customers in Kenya, with our initial launch targeting Nairobi and its surrounding areas.
